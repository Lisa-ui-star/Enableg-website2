Enable G: Empowering Lives Through UpSkilling

Enable G is a non-profit organisation founded by David Hay in 2021 after his retirement as a businessman and entrepreneur. It started in Cape Town, South Africa, and has now expanded to Bali and across South and Central America.

Our mission is to help individuals improve their lives by developing personal and business skills. We offer guidance and resources to support both personal and career growth. Young adults, in particular, benefit from learning life skills while engaging with people from different countries and cultures.

For more information or to get involved, please contact us at info@enableg.com.

David Hay
